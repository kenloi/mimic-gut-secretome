Gut secretome × human receptor screen

MIMIC — a mobile microbial peptide that engages a human cytokine receptor

A structure-first screen of the gut secretome: ~2,000 gut-secreted miniprotein families co-folded against human immune receptors and sequence-matched decoys, every interface scored against a null. The lead hit lands on the exact IL-7 binding surface of IL7R with no sequence homology — a structural mimic.

Kenneth Loi · Zhaojun Wang — built with Claude Science
